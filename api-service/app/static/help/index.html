Help: Using the API Service

1. Registering for an Account

  1. Go to the registration page by clicking on the "Create an account" link.
  2. Enter your email and password. Confirm your password.
    The password must be at least 8 characters long.
  3. Check the box to agree to our Privacy Policy.
  4. Click the "Register" button to create your account.
  5. You will be notified of your account creation in the email you provided.
  6. You can now return to ligin page and log in to your account.
  7. To get access to models you need to contact administrator.

2. Changing Your Password

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Change your password in the user page.
  3. Enter your current password, new password and confirm new password.
  4. Click the "Change Password" button to change your password.
  5. You will be notified of your password change in your email.

3. Deleting your Account

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Click on the "Delete" button to open the account deletion form.
  3. Enter your password to confirm your intention to delete your account.
  4. Check the box to agree to the deletion of your account.
  5. Click the "Delete" button to delete your account.

Note: Deleting your account will permanently remove all your data from our system.
Please make sure you want to delete your account before proceeding.

4. Token management

  1. You must be registred to make token.
  2. Get public key from the public key api endpoint.
  3. Encrypt your password and email with the public key using RSA algorithm.
  4. For web-js you can use our completed RSA file and fetch HttpOnly token from web-token api endpoint.
  5. For python you should use PyCryptodome library and SHA256 algorithm
    and get not HttpOnly token from token api endpoint.
  6. Use POST method to send encrypted credentials to token api endpoint you choose.
  7. If your token is in cookies use GET method.
  8. If you include token in request body use POST method. Use "token" name in request body.
  9. For more information look to api endpoints docs.
  10. Token is valid for 3 hours.
